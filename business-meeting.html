Business Meeting Program

Structured access to
Vietnam's top aesthetic clinics

The Business Meeting Program gives Korean medical device companies direct, scheduled 1:1 sessions with Vietnamese hospital directors, clinic owners, and purchasing managers attending K-Beauty MEETS events. KBIT facilitates the matching and scheduling.

🤝

Facilitated 1:1 Matching

KBIT reviews your company profile and meeting objectives, then matches you with pre-qualified Vietnamese buyers — hospital directors, clinic owners, and medical equipment purchasing managers actively attending the event.

📋

Scheduled & Pre-confirmed

30-minute dedicated sessions during lunch breaks and coffee breaks at each event. All meetings pre-confirmed by both parties. Dedicated interpreter available. On-site LOI signing facilitated for serious purchase intent.
