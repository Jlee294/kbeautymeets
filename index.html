Why K-Beauty MEETS?

Korean aesthetic medicine leads the world. K-Beauty MEETS gives Vietnamese doctors direct access to the people, protocols, and devices driving that standard — not through textbooks, but through hands-on experience in their own city.

KAT

Korea's training series —
expanding across Asia

KAT — K-Beauty Advanced Skills Training — brings Korean KOL directly to local doctors for real hands-on skills transfer. Each year, a new Asian city. Each edition, a deeper network of Korean-trained aesthetic professionals.

KAT 2025 trained 350 doctors, generated $1.34M in export consultations, and was recognized by Vietnam's Ministry of Health as a model of medical cooperation. KAT Vietnam 2026 builds on that foundation.

Free to attend

All K-Beauty MEETS events are fully free for Vietnamese medical professionals. Supported by KHIDI — the Korean Health Industry Development Institute — and organized by KSAPS, SNUBH, VSAPS, and KBIT.

Limited seats · Application review

Seats are limited. Applications reviewed by KBIT within 3–5 business days. Priority to specialist physicians (aesthetic surgery, dermatology, plastic surgery).

No-show policy

If you cannot attend, email secretary@kbitassociation.com in advance. Unannounced no-shows are recorded and affect priority at future KSAPS and KAT events.
