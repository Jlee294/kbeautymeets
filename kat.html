What makes KAT different

Skills, not slides.

KAT Vietnam 2026 is deliberately different from the Congress happening the day before. Where Congress delivers academic breadth across three rooms, KAT goes deep in one direction: you leave having done it — not just having heard about it.

Live surgery in real time

Room B broadcasts 4 live plastic surgery cases directly from the OR. Each case has step-by-step commentary from the operating Korean surgeon, with real-time Q&A from the audience. You're not watching a recording — you're watching it happen.

Rhinoplasty · Blepharoplasty · SVF & Fat Grafting · Mini Facelift — all with OR live broadcast

Hands-on with direct Korean KOL guidance

Room A runs small-group hands-on sessions throughout the day — one Korean KOL for every five Vietnamese doctors. Each group gets direct parameter guidance for RF/HIFU devices and hands-on Botox and filler injection on models. Not demonstration — actual practice.

The same 1:5 format used in KAT 2025 — recognized by Vietnam's Ministry of Health as a model of Korea-Vietnam medical cooperation

Day 2 of a 2-day journey

KAT Vietnam is designed as the direct follow-on to KSAPS Congress (Oct 17–18). Doctors who attend both leave with two days of the most concentrated Korean aesthetic education available in Vietnam — separate CME certificates for each event.

Congress + KAT combo registrants receive higher review priority and separate CME certificates for both events
