What is KSAPS Congress?

A full day of
Korean aesthetic medicine

KSAPS Congress 2026 is the centerpiece of K-Beauty MEETS — a full-day medical congress bringing together over 1,000 Vietnamese healthcare professionals with 15 of Korea's leading aesthetic surgeons and doctors.

Unlike a conventional conference, KSAPS Congress runs three rooms simultaneously, so delegates can choose the track most relevant to their practice — medical devices, aesthetic surgery, or Korean clinical protocols. This is the most comprehensive Korean aesthetic medical event ever held in Vietnam.

3 Parallel rooms — choose your track

Room A covers medical devices and energy-based technology. Room B focuses on aesthetic surgery. Room C delivers Korean clinical protocols for injectables and non-invasive treatments.

K-Innovation Pavilion

Exhibition zone with 15+ Korean medical device companies. B2B matching area, KOL-certified user programs, and LOI signing for distribution agreements.

VIP Gala Dinner

Exclusive networking dinner for 150–200 guests the evening of October 17. Priority given to specialist physicians and Congress + KAT combo registrants.

CME Certificate

KSAPS Congress 2026 certificate issued by KSAPS & KBIT. CME Certificate issued after the event.
