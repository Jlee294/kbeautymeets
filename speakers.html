VSAPS — The gateway
to Vietnam's aesthetic surgeons

The Vietnam Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgery (VSAPS) is the official Vietnamese partner for K-Beauty MEETS 2026. Founded in 2007 and recognized by Vietnam's Ministry of Health, VSAPS is the central organizing body for aesthetic surgery in Vietnam.

The KSAPS-VSAPS partnership MOU was signed at APS Korea 2026 in April — with VSAPS president Dr. Lê Hành and 23 VSAPS board members and representatives attending the ceremony in Seoul. This gives K-Beauty MEETS direct access to Vietnam's entire aesthetic surgery community.
